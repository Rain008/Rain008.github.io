Donkkaseu

Donkkaseu is the Korean interpretation of Japenese tonkatsu- a breaded pork cutlet. It is usually served with rice and cabbage salad.What is donkkaseu?

Ingredients:

  • pork loin
  • flour
  • eggs
  • bread crumbs
  • oil
  • preffered seasoning
  • rice

Directions:

  1. Make rice, how you usually make it.

  2. Get your pork loin. Then, pound and flatten the pork to the size desired by using a meat tenderizer or the back of a kitchen knife.

  3. Get the eggs, flour and the breadcrumbs in a seperate bowl each. If desired, put your preffered seasoning in the eggs.

  4. Cover the pork completely in the following order: eggs, flour and the bread crumbs.

  5. Once they are all covered, get some oil into a pan or skillet and start frying, until both sides are golden brown. And you are done!

*You can find donkkaseau sauce in most East Asian stores, or look up a recipe online.
